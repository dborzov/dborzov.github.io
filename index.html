Peter Borzov

Hi, I am Peter and here is where I keep my random stuff.

I like learning how things work and making new ones. I am interested in data analysis, machine learning and, more generally, using mathematics to understand the world better. I contribute to various open source projects, they are available at github.

I work as a data analyst at paykeeper.ru and live in Vancouver, BC.

Open source

Check out some of my open source projects:

  • Fredholm.py, is a Python library for solving Fredholm/integral equations numerically. Developed with problems in quantum many-body physics in mind, it is the only one, to the extent of my knowledge, that treats divergencies of principal value integrals properly.
  • subtitles2 / shrubbery, indexing of movie/tv show subtitles for word frequencies and quotes.
  • Machine learning, algorithm implementations and homework solutions for the Caltech online course on machine learning.
